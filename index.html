The Meta Simulacrum Vol. 1

The Meta Simulacrum is a deeply collaborative and extensive alternate reality (aka MetaVerse) composed of interlocking music, text, and video. Content in the MetaSim is linked together by its own indigenous Wiki System and will be supported by a decentralized, equitable blockchain. There is no defining form of the MetaSim - it is an organic and open-ended exercise in collective imagination.

Thus far, the artistic team includes William Brittelle (composer/creator), Isaac Gale (filmmaker), Patrick Marschke (coding, editing), Holland Andrews, Channy Leaneagh (Poli√ßa), Will Johnson (WILLS), Jenn Wasner (Wye Oak, Flock of Dimes, Bon Iver), Eliza Bagg (Lisel), Immanuel Wilkins (sax), Ian Chang (percussion), Erika Dohi (synth), Chris Fishman (keys), Rafiq Bhatia (guitar), Jackson Hill (bass), Roomful of Teeth, Constellation Chor, Metropolis Ensemble, and musicians of the Cincinnati Symphony Orchestra. The MetaSim community of creators will continue to expand as new content is developed.

Rather than centering on traditional live events and commercial releases, the MetaSim is structured around the release of volumes of interrelated content. Vol. 1, a 90-minute "opening tour" film, was co-presentation of Liquid Music, The Great Northern, Walker Art Center and Cincinnati Symphony Orchestra in partnership with the Metropolis Ensemble. Vol. 2 will center on the unveiling of the full metasimulacrum.com website.
